## Introduction

### What is ETIAS Assist?

ETIAS Assist is a guided preparation platform designed to help travelers determine their eligibility for the European Travel Information and Authorisation System (ETIAS) and prepare all required information before submitting their application on the official EU website.

### Important Disclaimer

> **ETIAS Assist is NOT the official EU ETIAS application portal.** We are an independent assistance service that helps you prepare your application. The actual ETIAS application must be submitted through the official EU website at [https://travel-europe.europa.eu/etias](https://travel-europe.europa.eu/etias).

### What is ETIAS?

ETIAS (European Travel Information and Authorisation System) is a visa waiver program for citizens of visa-exempt countries traveling to the Schengen Area. Starting in 2025, eligible travelers will need to obtain ETIAS authorization before entering Europe.

### Key Features

- **Eligibility Verification**: Instantly check if you need ETIAS authorization
- **Guided Form Completion**: Step-by-step assistance with all required fields
- **Real-time Validation**: Catch errors before they cause problems
- **Secure Data Handling**: Your information is encrypted and protected
- **AI-Powered Help**: Get instant answers to your questions
- **Progress Saving**: Resume your application anytime

1. Navigate to **"Eligibility Check"** from the main menu
2. Answer the following questions:

## Application Process

### Overview

The application process consists of 5 steps:

1. **Personal Information** - Basic identity details
2. **Passport Details** - Travel document information
3. **Travel Information** - Trip details and accommodation
4. **Background Questions** - Security and health questions
5. **Review & Confirm** - Final verification

### Step 1: Personal Information

Required fields:
- First name (as shown on passport)
- Last name (as shown on passport)
- Date of birth
- Place of birth
- Gender
- Nationality
- Email address
- Phone number

**Tips:**
- Enter your name exactly as it appears on your passport
- Use your current, active email address
- Double-check all spelling

### Step 2: Passport Details

Required fields:
- Passport number
- Country of issue
- Issue date
- Expiry date

**Important:**
- Your passport must be valid for at least 3 months beyond your planned stay
- Passport must be machine-readable (biometric)
- Enter the passport number without spaces or dashes

### Step 3: Travel Information

Required fields:
- First EU country of entry
- Intended address in EU (hotel, accommodation)
- Purpose of travel
- Planned arrival date
- Planned departure date

**Tips:**
- If visiting multiple countries, enter the first country you'll arrive in
- You can provide hotel booking details or a general address
- Be accurate with your travel dates

### Step 4: Background Questions

You'll be asked about:
- Previous travel to conflict zones
- Criminal history
- Immigration violations
- Health conditions (if applicable)
- Previous visa refusals

**Important:**
- Answer all questions truthfully
- False information can result in application denial
- Most travelers answer "No" to these questions

### Step 5: Review & Confirm

- Review all entered information
- Make corrections if needed
- Confirm accuracy of your application
- Proceed to payment

### Saving Your Progress

- Your application is automatically saved after each step
- You can close the browser and return later
- Access saved applications from your Dashboard
- Draft applications are stored securely

## Submitting to Official EU Website

### After Payment

Once payment is complete:

1. You'll see the **Success** page
2. Review your prepared application summary
3. Click **"Go to Official EU ETIAS Website"**
4. A new tab opens with the official portal

### What to Do on the Official Website

1. Start a new ETIAS application
2. Use the information you prepared with us
3. Copy details from your summary
4. Complete the official form
5. Pay the official €7 ETIAS fee
6. Submit your application

### Important Notes

> **We do NOT submit applications on your behalf.** You must complete the final submission yourself on the official EU website. This ensures your application is legitimate and directly processed by EU authorities.

### After Submission

- Most applications are approved within minutes
- Some may take up to 96 hours
- Complex cases may require up to 30 days
- You'll receive the decision via email from EU authorities

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is ETIAS Assist the official EU website?**
A: No, we are an independent assistance service. The official application must be submitted at travel-europe.europa.eu/etias.

**Q: Why should I use ETIAS Assist?**
A: We help you prepare your application correctly, reducing errors and rejections. Our guided process and AI assistance make the process easier.

**Q: How long is ETIAS valid?**
A: ETIAS authorization is valid for 3 years or until your passport expires, whichever comes first.

**Q: Can I use ETIAS for multiple trips?**
A: Yes, once approved, you can use it for unlimited entries during the validity period.

### Application Questions

**Q: What if I make a mistake on my application?**
A: You can edit your application at any step before payment. After submission to the EU, you may need to apply again.

**Q: How long does the preparation take?**
A: Most users complete the preparation in 10-15 minutes.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

**Q: Is this in addition to the official ETIAS fee?**
A: Yes, the official €7 ETIAS fee is paid separately on the EU website.
